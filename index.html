About Mumbai

  • Mumbai (formerly called Bombay) is a densely populated city on India’s west coast. A financial center, it's India's largest city.
  • On the Mumbai Harbour waterfront stands the iconic Gateway of India stone arch, built by the British Raj in 1924.
  • Offshore, nearby Elephanta Island holds ancient cave temples dedicated to the Hindu god Shiva. The city's also famous as the heart of the Bollywood film industry.
